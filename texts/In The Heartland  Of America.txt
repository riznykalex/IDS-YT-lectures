"Hi everybody. I just finished watching this beautiful, heartfelt film. It was a spiritual experience: music, song, dance, story, from the costumes to the depth of going beyond just being human. I cried, I laughed, I was scared, I rejoiced. It is an amazing experience, and it just feels like a blessing to have been a part of seeing this in an audience. My heart is cracked wide open. I am an open person, but to watch these performers perform from their heart—where it did not even feel like performing, it felt like them just being themselves on stage, showing their colors and personas and everything in such an open, brilliant, loving way—really touched my heart. I cried through the entire performance because I felt my heart connection with every performer on the stage, and I felt such joy at the universal connection that we all share. I am so appreciative of that, and I cannot wait to experience this again. I hope I have the opportunity soon.

Okay, I saw the show and I absolutely loved it. It was tremendous. I met the master of ceremonies the other night at this preview. I thought I loved variety shows, I love cultural dancing, I love everything. The show was great on all levels: the costumes, the choreography, the concepts.
