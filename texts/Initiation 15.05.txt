"Now we will ask His Grace Caturātma Prabhu to officiate the initiation ceremony. We won't have a fire yajña because you can't burn anything in this building. However, even without the yajña, the significance of the ceremony will not diminish. It is still very important. Of course, we have many different rituals that accompany the ceremony, but the most important thing is what is in our hearts.

A disciple surrenders to the spiritual master, which means he surrenders to the entire guru-paramparā, and in exchange, he gets the shelter of not only the spiritual master but the entire paramparā. Therefore, what is happening today is a full-fledged initiation. But if you have the opportunity in the coming weeks or months, be sure to participate in the fire yajña and offer grains to the fire. And now Caturātma Prabhu will explain to you what to do. You will perform ācamana, take your vows, and when the time comes, you will receive such wonderful names. We have selected the best names today. If I were in the place of these young men and women who are being initiated, I would be simply crazy about my new name. And of course, for most devotees, this is the most joyful part of the ceremony, when you receive a new name and your friends applaud, hug you, and congratulate you. So please keep the mṛdaṅga and karatālas ready to welcome the new members of the ISKCON and Śrīla Prabhupāda family. Before we begin, I would like to take this opportunity to say a few words to those who are being initiated.

You have been waiting for this opportunity for many millions of lives. You have been people, birds, and reptiles. Some of you were fish too, because there are 8,400,000 different forms of life, but now you are finally human. There is no doubt that the vows that you offer to His Holiness Indradyumna Swami, and this is the most important step that connects you with the paramparā and with our sampradāya. And this is the real beginning of your life. But please remember that this is just the beginning of your life, not the end.

There are always different types of contaminations in our lives that make us ineligible to participate in any Vedic ceremony. But remembering the lotus-eyed Lord frees us from all kinds of defilement, and we can act. Everyone has new kaṇṭhī-mālā. Don't wear the kaṇṭhī-mālā yourself. Let someone sitting next to you help you. ### Closing Address by Badahari Prabhu
It's a very happy day. I am very happy to see the shining faces of the children, shining with Kṛṣṇa consciousness. And if we had transcendental vision, I am sure we would see many demigods showering their blessings upon the people of Muni, Lord Śiva. Now you have the blessings of the entire paramparā and all the divine personalities. Therefore, you have everything—all the power to move forward. I also pray for your spiritual advancement and for you to have strength in your spiritual life. 

As Śrīla Prabhupāda said, chanting Hare Kṛṣṇa is the most important instruction of the spiritual master. And I beg you, don't take this superficially. He said that even if we chant 16 rounds, Kṛṣṇa pulls us by the hand. As Mahārāja said, chanting should always be a priority in our lives. Serious and sustained efforts to chant—serious chanting is *nāmābhāsa*. If we make a serious effort every day to sit down and chant our rounds mindfully, through that effort our heart will become purified. 

And the second is to learn to love and serve devotees. If you learn how to properly serve devotees and treat them with love, you will progress very quickly in spiritual life. Because we have seen even very powerful devotees give up their practice because of offenses to devotees. Due to offenses, we also lose our taste for chanting. These are two very important principles that the Lord presented to us: reading the books and serving the Vaiṣṇavas. And another principle is to be merciful and share good fortune with others. Naturally, Kṛṣṇa will also be merciful to you. Thank you for taking this bold step. Śrīla Prabhupāda said that devotees have shining faces. We are very happy to see your shining faces and we pray for your success.""
