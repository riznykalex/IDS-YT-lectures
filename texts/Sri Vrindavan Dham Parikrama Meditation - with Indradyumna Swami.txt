"Meditate on the abode with intense love. Roll your entire body in the dust of Vṛndāvana. Offer your material body in the service of Vṛndāvana and thus achieve firm love of God. Worship the beloved Lord of Śrī Rādhā and satisfy all animate and inanimate living entities. In this way, take shelter of Vṛndāvana with your body, mind, and speech.

Alas, even after considering the purport of the statements of the Upaniṣads for a long time, one cannot obtain a tinge of transcendental sweetness; yet, simply by residing in Śrī Vṛndāvana, one can relish the ocean of that transcendental sweetness. May that Vṛndāvana become manifest to me.

May the abode of Vṛndāvana be glorified. The transcendental glories of a single particle of its dust minimizes the glories of innumerable kāmadhenu or surabhi cows, desire trees, and touchstones. Even the best of demigods like Brahmā, Śiva, and Lakṣmī never know the glories of such a particle of dust.

Just by living in Vṛndāvana, all one's faults are automatically destroyed. It turns sinful miscreants into highly qualified, religious persons. My only prayer is that the abode of Vṛndāvana may counteract all my sinful reactions and maintain me, for I am fallen from all religious principles.

Let my eyes become overwhelmed watching the beautiful, nectarean waves of the forest of Vṛndāvana. Let my intelligence become merged in the nectarean ocean of the glories of Vṛndāvana, and let my body roll on the ground of Vṛndāvana due to being absorbed in the current of spiritual bliss. O, may I always offer obeisances at the feet of all living entities who reside in Vṛndāvana.""
