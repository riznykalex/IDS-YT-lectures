"Hare Kṛṣṇa, dear friends and well-wishers. This is Indradyumna Swami, hoping this message finds you in the best of health and spirits. 

I’m happy to announce that soon I will be embarking on a special tour throughout India with my latest book, *The Sacred Forests of Vṛndāvana*, situated on the western bank of the Yamunā. It is actually the first in a series of books on the glories of Vṛndāvana, compiled from my lectures throughout the years. It includes descriptions of a number of Vṛndāvana’s holy places and divine personalities, as well as many of the pastimes of God and Kṛṣṇa, along with Their associates. 

Śrīla Prabhupāda writes in his *Kṛṣṇa* book: "Vṛndāvana is such a nice place; flowers are always blooming, and there are even various kinds of decorated deer. Birds are chirping, peacocks are crowing and dancing, and bees are humming. The cuckoos there sing nicely in five kinds of tunes."

In essence, this well-researched book, along with many beautiful photos of the dhāma, is a veritable pilgrimage to Vṛndāvana, the playground of God. It can act as a guidebook on your next visit to Vṛndāvana, or simply as a voyage to that sacred land, even while sitting in your own home. I hope this book will deepen your attachment to Śrī Vṛndāvana. 

I’m looking forward to meeting all of you and personally signing each and every copy. All glories to Śrīla Prabhupāda, the revealer of the dhāma. Hare Kṛṣṇa, see you soon. 

Your servant, 
Indradyumna Swami""
